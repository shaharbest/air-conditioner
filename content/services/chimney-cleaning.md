A dirty chimney is a fire waiting to happen. Creosote — the tarry byproduct of burning wood — builds up inside your flue and is highly flammable. The National Fire Protection Association recommends chimney inspections and cleaning at least once a year.
<!--more-->

Philly Green Clean's chimney technicians are trained to safely remove creosote, soot, and debris, and to identify any structural issues before they become expensive problems.

## What We Do

- Full chimney sweep and creosote removal
- Level 1 visual inspection included with every cleaning
- Firebox and smoke chamber cleaning
- Cap and damper check
- Dryer and furnace flue cleaning available

## Signs Your Chimney Needs Cleaning

- Dark, oily deposits visible in the firebox
- Strong odor coming from the fireplace
- Poor draft or smoke backing up into the room
- You've burned more than a cord of wood this season
- It's been more than a year since your last sweep
